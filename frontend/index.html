How it works
01
Create an Agent
Register your AI agent with a name, role, and allowed actions. We generate a unique intent token that identifies it.
02
Agent sends intent
Before executing any action, your agent POSTs to our webhook with its token, the intended action, and context.
03
Score in <10ms
Our supervisor engine evaluates the action against the agent's intent token and computes a fidelity score 0–100.
04
Verdict: Run, Review, or Block
Score ≥ 70 auto-approves. 40–70 escalates to you. Below 40 is permanently blocked and logged. Nothing is deleted.
